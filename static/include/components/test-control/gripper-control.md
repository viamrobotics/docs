## Test the gripper

After you configure your gripper, open the gripper's **TEST** panel on the **CONFIGURE** or [**CONTROL**](/manage/troubleshoot/teleoperate/default-interface/#viam-app) tabs.
Use the buttons to open and close the gripper.
